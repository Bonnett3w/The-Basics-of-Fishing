Rivers and Streams

Ocean

These are still bodies of fresh water that you can fish from a boat or from the shore. Easy to fish because of how accessible they are, and the variety of different wasy you can fish them.

Moving bodies of fresh water, you can fish slow moving rivers by boat or from the shore. Faster moving rivers and streams are only fishable from the shore. They are great places to catch hard fighting fish, but also offer the unique challenge of moving water

The ocean is a very vast space to fish. Unlike ponds, rivers, streams and lakes, the ocean is a body of saltwater, so the fish that live in the ocean are for the most part different from those that live in freshwater and you don't really find these two species in the opposite body. You can fish from the shore or from a boat in shallow or deep water
